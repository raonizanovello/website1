The Standard

Most modern fitness is focused on "more." We focus on "enough." The Standard represents the baseline of functional strength required to navigate the world with total structural integrity.

10 Strict Pull-ups
20 Strict Push-ups
30 Full Squats

Why this matters

Hitting these benchmarks ensures you have the relative strength to protect your joints, the mobility to move through full ranges of motion, and the foundation to specialize in any sport—from marathon running to powerlifting.

If you can't hit The Standard, you are training on a fragile foundation.
