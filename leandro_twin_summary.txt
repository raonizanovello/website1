# Summary: Leandro Twin - Consultoria Esportiva

## Biography (Sobre o Leandro Twin)
Leandro Twin is a fitness professional with over 15 years of experience helping people achieve their physical goals. His career was born from a deep personal transformation: he overcame obesity, losing nearly 40kg and subsequently gaining over 20kg of muscle mass. He operates both online and in-person (Moema, São Paulo), emphasizing a science-based, empathetic, and highly individualized approach to training and nutrition.

## Services (Assessoria e Consultoria)
Leandro offers personalized sports consultancy focused on maximum hypertrophy and fat loss. Key features include:
- Personalized Training & Diet: 100% custom plans built by Leandro himself (no AI or generic templates) based on the student's history, metabolism, and routine.
- Direct Support: Daily access to Leandro Twin for questions (Monday to Thursday) and bi-weekly (quinzenal) follow-ups to adjust the plan.
- Assessoria Online: A digital coaching service with flexible plans (Monthly, Quarterly, Semestral, Annual, and Biannual).
- Assessoria Presencial: In-person consultations in Moema, SP, or via video call for a more personal interaction.
- Support Materials: Includes instructional videos for correct exercise execution and additional guides for nutrition and training.
- Satisfaction Guarantee: A 30-day refund policy if the service does not meet the proposed standards.

## Courses (Cursos)
While the main landing page highlights the consultancy services, Leandro Twin also offers specialized courses. These are aimed at providing deeper knowledge on fitness, health, and body evolution, though specific course titles and details are hosted on a dedicated section of the site.
