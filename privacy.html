Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy describes what information Base Level Strength collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. If you have questions, email hello@baselevelstrength.org.

Who we are

Base Level Strength is a strength and conditioning coaching practice based in San Francisco, California, operated by Ronnie. Our website is located at baselevelstrength.org.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Email address — when you submit a form to request the free training template or subscribe to the newsletter.
  • Booking information — name, email, and scheduling preferences when you book a call via Cal.com.

We do not collect payment information directly. If you become a coaching client, payments are handled through a separate platform.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the free PDF template you requested.
  • To send occasional training essays and coaching updates (the newsletter).
  • To follow up on a booking inquiry you initiated.

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This site uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Formspree (formspree.io) — processes form submissions including email capture. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Cal.com (cal.com) — handles scheduling for intro calls. See Cal.com's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces from Google's servers. Google may log requests. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics (if enabled) — collects anonymized usage data to help understand how the site is used. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services (Cal.com, Google Analytics) may set their own cookies when you interact with them.

Your rights

  • Unsubscribe — every email includes an unsubscribe link.
  • Access or deletion — email hello@baselevelstrength.org to request access to or deletion of your data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@baselevelstrength.org
